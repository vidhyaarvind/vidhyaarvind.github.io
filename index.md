    <h1>Hi, I'm Vidhya 👋</h1>
    <p>
      I'm a Staff Software Engineer, Data Abstractions at <strong>Netflix</strong>
      and a founding architect of Netflix's <strong>Data Abstraction Platform</strong>
      — the layer that powers KeyValue, TimeSeries, Tree, Graph, and Table Metadata
      storage for thousands of internal developers and hundreds of mission-critical
      use cases.
    </p>
    <p>
      I work on distributed databases, write-ahead logging, data deletion at
      scale, and reliable APIs that hide a lot of complexity. Before Netflix,
      I built systems at <strong>Box</strong> and <strong>Verizon</strong>.
    </p>
    <p>
      I write on the
      <a href="https://netflixtechblog.com/introducing-netflixs-key-value-data-abstraction-layer-1ea8a0a11b30">Netflix TechBlog</a>
      and speak about data abstractions and platform engineering at QCon,
      Cassandra Summit, Austin API Summit, and Future of Memory &amp; Storage.
      You can find me on
      <a href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/vidhya-arvind-11908723/">LinkedIn</a>,
